# Astrology

The study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs and the natural world. Astrology is an ancient practice that seeks to understand and predict personality traits, life events, and destinies based on the positions of celestial objects at the time of a person's birth or a significant event.

## Core Components
- **Planets:** Represent different drives and functions within the human psyche.
- **Zodiac Signs:** Twelve divisions of the ecliptic, each associated with specific personality traits and archetypes.
- **Houses:** Twelve divisions of the sky, each representing different areas of life (e.g., relationships, career, family).
- **Aspects:** The angular relationships between planets, indicating how their energies interact.

## Zodiac Signs
- There are twelve zodiac signs, each corresponding to a specific period of the year and associated with unique characteristics. These are grouped by element (Fire, Earth, Air, Water) and modality (Cardinal, Fixed, Mutable).
- **Examples:** Aries (Fire, Cardinal), Taurus (Earth, Fixed), Gemini (Air, Mutable), Cancer (Water, Cardinal), Leo (Fire, Fixed), Virgo (Earth, Mutable), Libra (Air, Cardinal), Scorpio (Water, Fixed), Sagittarius (Fire, Mutable), Capricorn (Earth, Cardinal), Aquarius (Air, Fixed), Pisces (Water, Mutable).

## Planetary Influences
- Each planet in astrology is associated with different energies and influences.
- **Examples:**
    - **Sun:** Ego, core identity, vitality.
    - **Moon:** Emotions, instincts, subconscious.
    - **Mercury:** Communication, intellect, learning.
    - **Venus:** Love, beauty, values, relationships.
    - **Mars:** Drive, aggression, action, passion.
