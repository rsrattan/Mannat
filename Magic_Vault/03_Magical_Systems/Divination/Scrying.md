# Scrying

Scrying is the practice of looking into a suitable medium in the belief that it will reveal messages or visions. The medium can be a crystal ball, a mirror, water, smoke, or other reflective surfaces.

## Methods of Scrying
- **Crystal Gazing:** Using a crystal ball to induce visions or receive messages.
- **Mirror Scrying:** Using a black mirror or a regular mirror in dim light to see images or symbols.
- **Hydromancy:** Scrying using water, often by observing reflections or patterns in a bowl of water.
- **Capnomancy:** Scrying using smoke, interpreting the shapes and movements of smoke from incense or a fire.

## Purpose
- Scrying is used for divination, seeking guidance, communicating with spirits, or gaining insight into hidden knowledge.
- It often involves entering a meditative or trance-like state to enhance perception and receptivity.
