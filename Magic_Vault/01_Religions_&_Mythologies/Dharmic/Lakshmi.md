# Lakshmi

Lakshmi is the Hindu goddess of wealth, fortune, power, beauty, and prosperity. She is one of the principal goddesses in Hinduism and is often worshipped as the consort of Vishnu.

## Symbolism

*   **Lotus Flower:** Lakshmi is often depicted seated or standing on a lotus flower, which symbolizes purity, spiritual power, and creation.
*   **Gold Coins:** She is frequently shown with gold coins flowing from her hands, representing material wealth and abundance.
*   **Elephants:** Elephants are often shown beside her, symbolizing royal authority and strength.
*   **Four Arms:** Her four arms represent the four goals of human life (purusharthas): Dharma (righteousness), Artha (wealth), Kama (desire), and Moksha (liberation).

## Meditation for Abundance

This meditation is designed to connect with the energy of Lakshmi and cultivate a mindset of abundance.

1.  **Preparation:** Find a quiet space and sit comfortably. You may light a candle or incense. It can be helpful to have an image or statue of Lakshmi to focus on.

2.  **Invocation:** Begin by chanting the simple mantra "Om Shreem Mahalakshmyai Namaha" to invoke her presence. Feel the vibration of the chant filling your space.

3.  **Visualization:** Close your eyes and visualize a radiant golden light entering the crown of your head and filling your entire body. Imagine yourself seated on a large, pink lotus flower in the middle of a serene lake.

4.  **Connecting with Lakshmi:** Visualize the goddess Lakshmi before you, seated on her own lotus, smiling benevolently. See a continuous stream of golden coins flowing from her hand into yours. Feel the energy of abundance, prosperity, and joy radiating from her.

5.  **Affirmation:** Silently or aloud, repeat affirmations such as: "I am open to receiving abundance," "I am a magnet for wealth and prosperity," "I am grateful for the wealth in my life."

6.  **Closing:** Thank Lakshmi for her blessings. Gently bring your awareness back to your body and the room. Carry the feeling of abundance with you throughout your day.
