# Hermeticism

A philosophical and religious tradition based upon the writings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus, a syncretic figure combining the Greek god Hermes and the Egyptian god Thoth.

## Core Texts
- **The Corpus Hermeticum:** A collection of 17 Greek writings whose authorship is traditionally attributed to Hermes Trismegistus.
- **The Kybalion:** A 20th-century book that summarizes Hermetic philosophy, outlining its seven core principles.
